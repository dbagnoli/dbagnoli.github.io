Endless Runner (VGDC at NCSU)



Download Link: Endless Runner

Description:

Endless Runner is a VR game for the Oculus Rift developed by the Video Game Developers Club at NC State. You awake aboard an abandoned spaceship destined for destruction. Escape if you can, but first you will have to make it through the ship. Destroy the crystals to get to the next door before the ship explodes.


Instructions:

Click the download link, download the folder, extract the contents, ensure the Oculus Store is open and your VR headset is plugged in and run the file VGDC_VR.exe. Use the triggers to shoot at the crystals on the door. Each crystal has a color that marks how many shots it will take to destroy it. The color will change as you hit it showing the door is closer to being open. Once all of the crystals on a door have been destroyed the door will open and allow you to continue. If you fail to destroy all of the crystal... Prepare for certain death!
