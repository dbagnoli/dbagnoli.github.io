Survival for the Fittest (Global Game Jam 2019)



Download Link: Survival for the Fittest

Description:

Play against your friends as your forest animal builds its home to survive natural disasters. Collect resources to build a home suitable for the disaster and outlast the other animals to win.


Instructions:

Click the download link, download the folder, extract the contents, and run the file godot_demo_windows.exe. To connect through LAN one computer must host. After choosing to host, the other players may input the host's IP address. The host may then begin the session. Use WASD to move around the forest. Birds can fly (for a limited amount of time), squirrels can climb trees and jump high, and foxes can enter the underground tunnel. Resources will randomly spawn and players can press E to pick them up.


Role:
