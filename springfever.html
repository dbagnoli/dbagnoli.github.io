Spring Fever (NCSU Game Jam)



Download Link: Spring Fever

Description:

As a nose in Spring, there is nothing you hate to see more than allergens popping up all over the place. Avoid the nasty throes of Spring and upgrade your nose to become unstoppable!


Instructions:

Click the download link, download the folder, extract the contents, and run the file SpringFever.exe. Use AD to move the nose left and right and press the space bar to jump. After upgrades have been unlocked they can be activated with RQE. Good luck making it out of Spring without sneezing!
