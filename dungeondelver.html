Dungeon Delver (Game Design Project)



Download Link: Dungeon Delver

Description:

Dungeon Delver Dan is dedicated to exploring dungeons and uncovering their mysteries and secrets. It is and always has been his life’s work. However, on one of his many trips to a dungeon, he came face to face with a horde of monsters! Unable to retreat, our valiant hero was knocked out and brought to the monster’s dungeon. When Dan wakes up, all of the monsters have gone out on a raid giving the dungeon delver time to escape from his cell. However, escaping will not be that easy. The monsters are very picky with their security. Due to this slight hiccup in Dan’s plans, Dan must use all of his creativity to escape from his dungeon and see the beautiful light of day once more.


Instructions:

Click the download link, download the folder, extract the contents, and run the file DungeonDelver.swf. Use the arrow keys to move around. Click on certain items to interact with them (these items are not marked in any way to show they are interactable). For hints on certain levels click the yellowish-orange question mark. You can also type on your keyboard to have text appear on screen. There is no need to press "Enter" after you finish typing a certain string of text. Check the readme included in the .zip folder for solutions.
