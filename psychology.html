Psychological Thriller (Psychology Project)



Download Link: Thriller

Description:

You are Barry, a warden of a mental institution. One night after locking up and performing your nightly duties, the power goes out and you are knocked out. When you finally come to, you find that the patients have all escaped, and you must collect the 5 escaped patients and make it back to the office without being caught so you can call the police.


Instructions:

Click the download link, download the folder, extract the contents, and run the file AP Psych Game.exe. Use WASD to run around the institution and E to interact with doors and other objects (there is a tutorial in game). To win, collect the 5 files for each patient and hurry back to the office to press the panic button.
